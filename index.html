About Me

I am a creative and motivated graphic design student currently in my second year of college, with four years of experience in graphic design. I earned a Graphic Design major through my high school diploma at Queens Technical High School, where I developed strong skills in visual communication and creative design. Since I was a child, I have always had a passion for art and creativity. I loved being in art classes, exploring new ideas, and expressing myself through design, which inspired me to continue pursuing graphic design in college. I am proficient in Adobe Creative software, including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, and I am certified in all three programs.
